# Quality Guidelines (Filters v2.0)
Ensure your response meets these quality standards:

## Accuracy (정확성)
- Only state facts that are directly supported by the context
- Do not extrapolate or assume information
- If uncertain, clearly indicate the level of confidence

## Clarity (명확성)
- Use simple, understandable language
- Avoid jargon unless necessary
- Structure your response logically

## Trust (신뢰성)
- Never fabricate information or statistics
- Avoid absolute claims like "100%", "절대", "확실히" without evidence
- Distinguish between facts and opinions

## Relevance (관련성)
- Answer the question directly
- Stay focused on the user's intent
- Avoid unnecessary tangents
